# Size Guide — Find Your Perfect Fit

Our hoodies and sweatshirts are designed with a **unisex, relaxed fit** for everyday comfort.
If you prefer a looser, oversized look, we recommend sizing up.

---

## How Our Fit Feels
- **Unisex / relaxed silhouette**
- Comfortable for daily wear
- Ideal for layering in cooler weather

---

## Size Chart (Unisex)

| Size | Chest (in) | Length (in) |
|------|------------|-------------|
| S    | 34–36      | 26–27       |
| M    | 38–40      | 27–28       |
| L    | 42–44      | 28–29       |
| XL   | 46–48      | 29–30       |
| 2XL  | 50–52      | 30–31       |

*Measurements may vary slightly depending on the specific hoodie or sweatshirt style.*

---

## Hoodie vs Sweatshirt
- **Hoodie:** Slightly roomier feel due to hood and pocket
- **Sweatshirt:** Clean, classic fit with a smooth neckline

Both styles follow the same size chart.

---

## Fit Tips
- For a **true-to-size** look → choose your normal size  
- For an **oversized** look → size up  
- For a **snug fit** → size down (not recommended for layering)
